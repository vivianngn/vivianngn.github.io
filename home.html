Musical-u is a platform I ideated and designed where people of all backgrounds and ages have the ability to learn how to play an instrument at any level without any cost, can view pre-recorded lessons with instrument-learning basics, can schedule live lessons with a teacher they can match with, and have unlimited access to a high quality, large database of sheet music for any instrument.

Hi! My name is Vivian Nguyen and I created this project in Digital Humanities 110 in Spring 2022. Previously Web UI Design @ Apple, incoming HMI Design @ Honda.

Farm2People

Farm2People was formed in April 2020, in response to the Covid-19 disruption of our food supply chain. Their goal is to address the problem we are all witnessing with the food supply chain and to form a more resilient food system for the future.

Program for Torture Victims

Program for Torture Victims (PTV) aims to rebuild the lives of survivors of torture and persecution from over 70 countries. PTV does this by connecting survivors to resources and rehabilitation to help restore their health and dignity.

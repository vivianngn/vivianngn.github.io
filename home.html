Farm2People was formed in April 2020, in response to the Covid-19 disruption of our food supply chain. Their goal is to address the problem we are all witnessing with the food supply chain and to form a more resilient food system for the future.

Program for Torture Victims

Program for Torture Victims (PTV) aims to rebuild the lives of survivors of torture and persecution from over 70 countries. PTV does this by connecting survivors to resources and rehabilitation to help restore their health and dignity.
